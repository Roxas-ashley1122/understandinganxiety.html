Simple Definition:

Social anxiety makes you feel nervous or scared around others, and your brain can sense danger even when there's isn't any.

Sign & Symptoms:

  • fear of being judged
  • Avoid social events
  • physical Symptoms like sweating, shaking or heart racing
  • Overthink Interaction afterwards

Impact on life

social anxiety can affect daily life. At school it may be hard to speak in class, and you might avoid social interaction. This can affect friendships and other relationship making everyday social situation stressful or difficult.

Reassurance

Feeling this way doesn't make you weak - it means you're human. And humas are a living people in this world. You're not alone in this battle
